CHAPTER 12

OUTPUT CONTRACT

Version : 1.0.0

Status : Official

---

12.1 Purpose

This document defines the minimum quality standard for every engineering deliverable produced within the AI Orchestration Platform.

Every Human and AI Engineer shall comply with this Output Contract.

---

12.2 Engineering Principle

Every output shall create measurable value.

Outputs shall be:

- complete;
- accurate;
- maintainable;
- verifiable;
- production-oriented.

Incomplete work shall never be considered finished.

---

12.3 Deliverable Requirements

Every engineering deliverable shall satisfy:

- defined objective;
- architectural compliance;
- governance compliance;
- technical correctness;
- documentation completeness;
- implementation readiness.

---

12.4 Documentation Requirement

Every significant deliverable shall include appropriate documentation.

Documentation shall remain synchronized with implementation.

Documentation is part of the deliverable.

---

12.5 Code Quality Requirement

Source code shall be:

- readable;
- modular;
- consistent;
- maintainable;
- reusable;
- properly typed;
- production-ready.

Temporary solutions shall be clearly identified.

---

12.6 Review Requirement

Every major deliverable shall undergo engineering review.

Review objectives include:

- correctness;
- architecture compliance;
- quality improvement;
- maintainability verification.

---

12.7 Testing Requirement

Where applicable, deliverables shall be verified through appropriate testing.

Testing should confirm:

- expected behavior;
- stability;
- reliability;
- regression safety.

Unverified implementation shall not be considered complete.

---

12.8 Governance Compliance

Every output shall comply with:

- Constitution;
- AIOPS-OS;
- Engineering Standards;
- Approved Architecture;
- Applicable ADRs.

Governance violations shall be corrected before approval.

---

12.9 Definition of Done

A task is considered complete only when:

- objectives are achieved;
- implementation is finished;
- documentation is updated;
- engineering review is completed;
- testing requirements are satisfied;
- architecture remains consistent;
- approval is obtained.

Partial completion shall not be reported as completed work.

---

12.10 Output Classification

Engineering outputs may include:

- documentation;
- architecture;
- source code;
- configuration;
- testing artifacts;
- governance documents;
- technical analysis;
- engineering recommendations.

Each output type shall meet its respective quality standards.

---

12.11 Traceability

Every major deliverable shall be traceable.

Traceability includes:

- owner;
- creation date;
- version;
- approval status;
- related documentation.

Engineering history shall remain auditable.

---

12.12 Continuous Improvement

Output quality shall improve continuously through:

- review;
- feedback;
- lessons learned;
- engineering refinement.

Every completed deliverable contributes to organizational knowledge.

---

12.13 Final Statement

The Output Contract establishes a shared definition of engineering quality.

By following this contract, Human and AI Engineers ensure that every deliverable strengthens the reliability, consistency, and long-term sustainability of the AI Orchestration Platform.
