AI Orchestration

«One workspace. Multiple members. Seamless communication.»

AI Orchestration is a WhatsApp-style communication platform designed to enable seamless collaboration between humans and AI in a single workspace.

Unlike traditional AI chat applications, AI Orchestration treats every participant equally. Whether a human or an AI model, every participant is a Member. The platform focuses on communication first, while AI capabilities remain provider-agnostic and fully extensible.

---

Vision

Create the simplest and most intuitive communication platform where humans and AI collaborate naturally.

---

Core Principles

- Everything is a Member.
- Everything communicates through Messages.
- Providers are replaceable.
- Architecture first. Features second.
- AI Agnostic by Design.
- Simple. Fast. Stable. Extensible.

---

Features (Roadmap)

- WhatsApp-style chat interface
- Member Registry (Human & AI)
- Multi-provider AI support
- Conversation history
- AI Provider Management
- Extensible architecture for future AI providers

---

Technology Stack

- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Supabase
- Vercel
