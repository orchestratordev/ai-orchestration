---

Purpose

This document records the official development history of the AI Orchestration Platform.

It provides a transparent and traceable record of architectural decisions, engineering milestones, and platform evolution.

---

Changelog Policy

Every significant change must be documented.

Changes should be:

- Accurate
- Chronological
- Meaningful
- Versioned
- Traceable

Minor formatting changes do not require a changelog entry unless they affect engineering decisions.

---

Versioning Strategy

The platform follows Semantic Versioning (SemVer).

Format:

MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH

Examples:

1.0.0

1.1.0

1.1.1

2.0.0

---

Version Definitions

MAJOR

Breaking architectural or platform changes.

MINOR

New features or significant enhancements.

PATCH

Bug fixes, documentation updates, minor improvements.

Deprecation Policy

- Be documented

Governance

- Honest
- Complete
- Easy to understand
